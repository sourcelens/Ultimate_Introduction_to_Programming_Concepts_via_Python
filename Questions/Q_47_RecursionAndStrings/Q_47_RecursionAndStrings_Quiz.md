1 : Examples of recursion in real world include _______  

a) Example of a tree   
b) Example of a flower   
c) Fibonacci series   
d) All of the above  

**Answer** d) 

**Description**

All are correct.A tree has branches that split into smaller branches, which in turn split into even smaller branches. This is a recursive pattern where each branch is a smaller version of the whole tree. Some flowers exhibit recursive patterns in the arrangement of their petals or seeds. The Fibonacci series is a classic example of a recursive sequence in mathematics.
